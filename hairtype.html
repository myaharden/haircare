Hair Type

Do you know your hair type?

Type 1: Straight

Type 1 hair consists of very straight and fine hair. Type 1 hair can also have some bend with a bit of a coarse texture.

Type 2: Wavy

Type 2 hair is wavy and fine. It has a more defined shape rather than type 1.

Type 3: Tight

Type 3 hair consists of faily loose curls but tighter than type 2 hair. It could also tight and springy.

Type 4: Kinky

Type 4 curls are tight and coily. These curls consist of zig zag curls and tight curls but tighter than type 3.
